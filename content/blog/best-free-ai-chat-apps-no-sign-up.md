## Tips for Maximizing Free Access

**Use multiple platforms.** Each platform's free tier resets independently. You can chat on Naviya in the morning, Character AI in the afternoon, and Chai in the evening.

**Create characters strategically.** If you're on a free tier, invest time in creating one great character rather than many mediocre ones. A well-crafted character with a detailed backstory produces better conversations.

**Save your best conversations.** Free tiers sometimes have conversation limits. Screenshot or copy your favorite exchanges before they're lost.

**Check for promotions.** Platforms regularly offer extended free trials or bonus credits for new users. Timing your sign-up with a promotion can double or triple your free access.
