## How to Stress-Test Message and Access Policies

Do a five-step audit before migrating your creative workflow:

1. **Signup drill**: Create a burner account on desktop and mobile. If any screen forces you onto a waitlist, bail.
2. **Burst test**: Send 50 rapid-fire prompts to measure how the app throttles responses. Naviya keeps latency steady, while some rivals slow drastically.
3. **Session length**: Keep a single conversation open for 48 hours. Apps with hidden caps often auto-archive after 24.
4. **Offline check**: Toggle airplane mode and reopen the app. Naviya caches recent conversations so you keep reading without data.
5. **Export rehearsal**: Download a transcript or character file. If an app locks data behind premium tiers, plan for eventual migration headaches.
